Privacy Policy – Fitcheck

Last updated: July 2026

Fitcheck is an app that lets users share outfit photos and have them rated by other users. This policy explains what data we collect and how we handle it.

What data we collect

  • Photos: the outfit photos you upload yourself.
  • Anonymous user ID: a randomly generated identifier (via Firebase Anonymous Authentication) to track which photos are yours. This ID is not linked to your name, email, or other personal information.
  • Votes: the number of hot/not votes per photo.
  • Screenshot notifications: whether a screenshot was taken of a photo in the feed, and how often.

How we use this data

Photos are shared in a joint feed so other users can rate them. We do not use any data for advertising and do not sell data to third parties.

Content moderation

Uploaded photos are automatically checked for inappropriate content via a third-party service (Sightengine). Borderline cases are additionally reviewed manually before becoming visible to other users.

Storage

Data is stored with Firebase (Google). Photos remain stored until you delete them yourself, or until an administrator removes them (for example after a valid report).

Your rights

You can delete your own photos at any time via the app. If you want all your data deleted, please contact us at the address below.

Age

Fitcheck is intended for users aged 17 and older.
